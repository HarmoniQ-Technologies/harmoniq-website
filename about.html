Our Mission

We Solved Electricity's Last Problem

For over a century, reactive power loss has been accepted as an unavoidable cost of industrial electricity. We changed that.

Our Story

Built to Fix What Everyone Else Ignored

Reactive power loss has existed since the first AC electrical network was built. For decades, the industry accepted it — passive capacitor banks offered partial, static compensation, but nobody had truly solved the problem of transforming wasted energy into useful power.

HarmoniQ was founded by a team of engineers, physicists, and industrial operators who refused to accept this status quo. After years of R&D, we developed a patented approach using precisely calibrated electromagnetic waveform injection — not to discard reactive power, but to transform it.

With 120+ sites, 1,240+ units, and 45 million operating hours behind us, the technology is proven. Now we're scaling globally — bringing perfect power quality to every energy-intensive facility on earth.
