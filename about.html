Our Mission

We Solved Electricity's Last Problem

For over a century, reactive power loss has been accepted as an unavoidable cost of industrial electricity. We changed that.

Our Story

Built to Fix What Everyone Else Ignored

Reactive power loss has existed since the first AC electrical network was built. For decades, the industry accepted it — passive capacitor banks offered partial, static compensation, but nobody had truly solved the problem of eliminating power quality waste at its source.

HarmoniQ was founded by a team of engineers and industrial operators who refused to accept this status quo. After years of R&D, we developed a patented Electrical Current Balancing System that cleans electrical current at every frequency — eliminating the distortions that cause waste before they occur.

With 30 years of research and development, 4,250+ units deployed, and 60 million operating hours behind us, the technology is proven. Now we're scaling globally — bringing perfect power quality to every energy-intensive facility on earth.
