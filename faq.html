The Technology

HarmoniQ injects precisely calibrated electromagnetic waveforms across your electrical network, transforming distortions (harmonics, reactive power) into usable energy. Unlike conventional power factor correction that simply discards reactive power, HarmoniQ converts it into productive work — maximising the value of every kilowatt-hour you buy.
Traditional capacitor banks compensate for reactive power passively and can only address fundamental frequency issues. They don't handle harmonics, and in some cases make harmonic problems worse. HarmoniQ uses active power electronics to dynamically transform reactive and harmonic energy into useful power — delivering vastly superior savings and addressing the full spectrum of power quality issues. It also adapts in real time to changing load conditions.
Yes. HarmoniQ's core electromagnetic waveform injection technology is protected by patents. This unique approach to transforming reactive energy is what sets us apart from all competing solutions and cannot be replicated by conventional power quality products.
HarmoniQ is compatible with all sub-600V electrical equipment — including induction motors, variable speed drives (VSDs/VFDs), UPS systems, HVAC chillers and air handling units, LED and fluorescent lighting systems, server and IT infrastructure, rectifiers and battery chargers, and all other linear and non-linear loads. It works with both single-phase and three-phase systems.
The IEEE Arrhenius Law of Thermal Degradation shows that motor insulation life doubles for every 10°C reduction in operating temperature. HarmoniQ reduces equipment operating temperatures by up to 20°C by eliminating the heat generated by harmonic currents and reactive power. This means a motor rated for 10 years could last 20–40 years under HarmoniQ conditions. The maintenance cost reduction and capital deferral this creates often exceed the direct energy savings.

Installation & Operations

HarmoniQ is installed in parallel with your existing electrical infrastructure — meaning your operations continue uninterrupted throughout the entire process. A typical installation takes 4–8 hours per unit and requires no rewiring or modifications to your existing equipment. Our certified engineers handle the full installation and commissioning.
Following a site assessment and agreement, most installations are completed within 2–4 weeks. This includes design, equipment manufacturing/configuration, and on-site commissioning. Larger or more complex multi-panel installations may take up to 6 weeks.
Yes. The HarmoniQ software platform integrates with most existing BMS, SCADA, and energy management systems via standard protocols (Modbus, BACnet, MQTT). Data from HarmoniQ can be exported to your existing dashboards, reports, and utility billing systems. Our team handles integration during commissioning.
HarmoniQ hardware is designed for continuous 24/7 operation with minimal maintenance. Annual inspection visits are recommended and included in rental agreements. The active power filter has no mechanical components subject to wear. Remote monitoring via the software platform flags any anomalies before they become issues.
