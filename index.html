The Problem

You're Paying For Power You Never Use

Every electrical facility experiences invisible losses. Harmonic distortions, reactive power, and poor power factor mean 10–25% of every electricity bill is pure waste — converted silently into heat.

Wasted Energy = Higher Bills

Poor power quality means you pay for reactive energy that does no useful work. Utilities charge penalties on top.

Equipment Overheats & Degrades

Harmonic distortions and excess heat stress motors, drives, and transformers — shortening lifespan by up to 50%.

Unnecessary Carbon Footprint

Every kilowatt-hour wasted is CO₂ emitted for nothing. Poor power quality silently inflates your Scope 2 emissions.

Your Electricity Bill — Where It Goes
Useful energy (productive work) 75 kW
Wasted as heat (reactive power) 25 kVAR
Power Factor Formula
PF = kW ÷ kVA = 0.75
You pay for 100 kVA. Only 75 kW does actual work.
Utilities charge penalties when power factor falls below 0.95
