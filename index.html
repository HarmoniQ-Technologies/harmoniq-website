The Problem

Inside Your Electrical Network

Clean power enters your facility at 50/60 Hz. By the time it reaches your equipment, harmonic distortion, reactive power, and voltage instability have turned up to 25% of it into waste heat — and you're billed for every watt.

Each connection carries distortion deeper into your network — costing energy, degrading equipment, and shortening its life.

10–25%

Of every bill is waste

Reactive current, harmonic losses, and demand charge inflation. You're paying for kWh and kVA that produce nothing but heat in your cables and transformers.

2–4×

Shorter equipment life

Every 10°C of excess operating temperature halves insulation life (IEEE Arrhenius). Poor power quality typically adds 15–20°C to motor windings.

85 t

CO2 emitted for nothing

A typical facility emits 85 tonnes of Scope 2 carbon per year purely from electrical waste (GHG Protocol Scope 2) — energy that was metered, billed, and did no productive work.

The Solution

How HarmoniQ Works

Our patented technology cleans your electrical current at every frequency — eliminating the distortions that cause waste before they cost you money.

01

Filters & Stabilises Waveforms

Eliminates harmonic distortions, stabilising current to a pure 50/60Hz sine wave across your entire network.

02

Eliminates Reactive & Harmonic Current

Removes the excess current that generates heat in cables, motors, and transformers — so your equipment draws only what it needs.

03

Eliminates utility penalty charges and reduces total apparent power demand.

04

Cuts Heat & Extends Equipment Life

Reducing operating temperature by up to 20°C extends motor and transformer lifespan by 2–4× (IEEE Arrhenius law).

05

Zero-Downtime Installation

Installed in parallel — no process interruption, no rewiring, no operational disruption.

06

Works With All Sub-600V Equipment

Compatible with motors, drives, servers, HVAC, lighting, and all linear and non-linear loads.
