Hardware

Three Devices. Complete Coverage.

HarmoniQ deploys as an integrated system across your full electrical network — selected and sized for your specific environment. Not a single panel fix. Complete, network-wide coverage.

HarmoniQ Filter

Software-controlled device that tracks and cancels up to 51 harmonic orders simultaneously — self-tuning in real time as your load profile changes throughout the day.

  • ✓ Dynamic real-time self-tuning
  • ✓ Cancels up to 51 harmonic orders
  • ✓ Scales with future load changes

HarmoniQ Alpha

Performs real-time impedance matching using patented hybrid resonant circuits — smoothing waveforms, reducing circulating currents, and lowering the total current drawn by equipment.

  • ✓ Targets specific harmonic frequencies
  • ✓ Compact, low-profile installation
  • ✓ Works in parallel — no downtime

HarmoniQ Booster

Corrects displacement power factor to 0.99+ using solid-state switching that replaces conventional capacitor banks — with zero resonance risk. Stabilises voltage and protects against sag and swell.

  • ✓ Corrects power factor to 0.99+
  • ✓ Stabilises voltage in real time
  • ✓ Ideal for motor-heavy operations

Our Patented Approach

We Don't Just Filter Symptoms — We Eliminate the Cause

Conventional power factor correction masks the problem at the meter — it does nothing for the excess current flowing through your facility's cables, motors, and transformers. HarmoniQ is different. Our patented Electrical Current Balancing System cleans the current throughout your entire network — so your equipment draws less electricity to do the same work.

The result: more usable energy from the same electricity bill, dramatically lower operating temperatures, and a network running at peak efficiency.

Measurement Methodology

How We Prove Every Result

We don't ask you to take our word for it. Every deployment follows a rigorous, independently verifiable measurement process.

Step 01

Baseline Period

Before installation, we log your energy consumption, power factor, and harmonic levels over a representative operating period — establishing a verified baseline.

Step 02

Parallel Installation

HarmoniQ hardware is installed in parallel — your operations continue without interruption. No shutdown. No disruption to production or facility operations.

Step 03

Live Metered Comparison

Savings are calculated by comparing metered consumption before and after — on the same load profile, same operating conditions. No modelling. No estimates.

Step 04

Customer Sign-Off

Results are presented to your energy team with full data. You validate the savings yourself before any commercial commitment is made.
