Is Your Site a Good Fit?

An ideal HarmoniQ site has one or more of these characteristics. The more that apply, the greater your savings.

✓

Annual electricity spend over $500k

The higher your spend, the larger the absolute savings.

✓

Significant inductive motor load

Motors, pumps, fans, compressors, and conveyors are major sources of reactive power.

✓

Power factor below 0.95

Utility penalties apply and reactive losses compound energy costs.

✓

Sub-600V linear and non-linear loads

Variable speed drives, UPS systems, servers, and rectifiers all generate harmonics.

✓

Total harmonic distortion (THD) above 5%

High THD indicates significant harmonic losses that HarmoniQ eliminates.

✓

Sites constrained on power capacity

Improving power factor effectively releases capacity — often avoiding costly infrastructure upgrades.
